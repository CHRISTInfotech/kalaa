About Us

Malayali Association of Delaware Valley, popularly know as Kalaa, was officially formed as
a community organization in October 1979. KALAA is an acronym for Kerala Art and Literary
Association of America.To bring together the Malayalees in the Delaware Valley and work
for the social, cultural and educational advancement of its members and the community at
large was the goal of the founding members of the group.

The beginning of Kalaa was the outcome of long discussions and negotiations. People worked together
in presenting the drama taunted the idea of starting a Malayalee association in Philadelphia.

After many meetings and prolonged discussion, the organization formed in October 1979.Today
Kalaa is one of the largest Malayalee Associations in North America with more than four hundred
members.True to its original spirit, Kalaa has made extraordinary services to the Malayalee community and the Indian community in general. Numerous cultural, literary and educational programs were coordinated by Kalaa. It also annually celebrated the major festivals of Kerala Conducting Malayalam schools, presenting Malayalam movies and presenting guest artists from Kerala are also in Kalaa’s activity list.
